RingIN Tones

RingIN Tones are high-quality music and audio tracks that you can play for people that call you. Instead of hearing beep beep beep they can listen to music of your choice. You can even customize your tones based on whos calling and when.

To use the service you need to activate the service either via SMS or IVR. Then you can customize your settings either by calling 387 or visiting http://ringintones.dialog.000webhostapp.com . Following the menus, you can choose what songs to play for callers, when, etc.

Activation

IVR : To activate dial 387 or visit ringintones.dialog.000webhostapp.com
SMS : Type RING & send to 678
USSD : Type #107# & send
